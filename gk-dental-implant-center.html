How an implant is actually placed

Four stages. No shortcuts.

Implant dentistry is a sequence, not a single visit — here's the exact order we follow with every patient at GK Dental & Implant Center.

01 · CONSULT

Exam & diagnosis

A full clinical exam and X-ray to understand bone density, gum health and bite alignment.

02 · PLAN

Digital scan & mapping

3D imaging pinpoints the exact depth, angle and position for the implant before any drilling begins.

03 · PLACE

Guided placement

The fixture is placed using a surgical guide built from your scan, under local anesthesia.

04 · RESTORE

Crown & aftercare

Once healed, a custom crown is fitted and matched to your bite and the surrounding teeth.
